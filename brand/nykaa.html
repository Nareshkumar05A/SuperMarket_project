Why Choose Flipkart?

Nykaa is India’s leading beauty, wellness, and lifestyle retailer, offering a curated range of 100% genuine products from top global and Indian brands. With a focus on quality and authenticity, Nykaa ensures that customers receive only original and trusted items.

Known for its expert-led product recommendations, easy returns, and user-friendly interface, Nykaa delivers a smooth and reliable shopping experience. It also provides influencer-driven tutorials, beauty advice, and personalized suggestions, making it more than just a shopping platform. With fast delivery, secure payments, and frequent sales, Nykaa is a favorite among beauty and wellness enthusiasts.

Nykaa was founded in 2012 by Falguni Nayar, a former investment banker, with the vision of creating a premium online destination for beauty and wellness in India. Headquartered in Mumbai, Nykaa has grown into a multibillion-dollar company and went public in 2021, becoming one of India’s most successful startups led by a woman entrepreneur.

Initially focused on cosmetics and skincare, Nykaa has now expanded into fashion, lifestyle, and personal care through platforms like Nykaa Fashion and Nykaa Man. The company follows an inventory-led model to ensure authenticity and has opened over 150 offline stores across the country to enhance the omnichannel experience.

COVID-19

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Nykaa adapted quickly by promoting hygiene products, masks, sanitizers, and wellness supplements. It ensured safe packaging, contactless deliveries, and implemented strict health protocols in warehouses. Despite operational challenges, Nykaa supported its workforce, suppliers, and customers through flexible policies, awareness campaigns, and timely service restoration.
