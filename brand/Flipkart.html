Why Choose Flipkart?

Flipkart offers a diverse range of products with fast delivery and reliable customer service across India. Its wide network ensures quick order fulfillment, even in remote areas, making it a top choice for online shopping.

With secure payments, regular offers, and a user-friendly return policy, Flipkart builds customer trust. Features like Flipkart Plus, personalized deals, and seamless app navigation enhance the experience. Flipkart also supports local sellers and promotes Make in India products, adding value to the economy.

Flipkart is one of India’s leading e-commerce companies, founded in October 2007 by Sachin Bansal and Binny Bansal in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Initially launched as an online bookstore, Flipkart quickly expanded into electronics, fashion, lifestyle, home essentials, and more—becoming a household name in India.

In 2018, Flipkart was acquired by Walmart, giving it a global boost while maintaining a strong Indian identity. It also owns and operates brands like Myntra, Cleartrip, and Shopsy, further expanding its digital reach.

COVID-19

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Flipkart played a crucial role in ensuring the delivery of essential goods across India, including groceries, medicines, and healthcare products. It implemented strict safety protocols in warehouses and delivery operations. Flipkart also supported its workforce by providing healthcare access and continued employment during lockdowns, helping meet rising demand in a responsible way.
