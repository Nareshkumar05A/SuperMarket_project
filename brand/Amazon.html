Why Choose Amazon ?

Amazon offers millions of products with fast, reliable delivery through its world-class logistics and Amazon Prime.

Its customer-first policies, secure payments, and easy returns make shopping smooth and trustworthy. Cutting-edge innovations like Alexa and personalized AI enhance the overall experience. Amazon also supports sustainability with eco-friendly practices and zero-emission goals.

Amazon is an American multinational technology company which focuses on e-commerce, cloud computing, and digital streaming. It has been referred to as "one of the most influential economic and cultural forces in the world", and is one of the world's most valuable brands.

Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos on July 5, 1994, in Bellevue, Washington, starting as an online bookstore. Over time, it evolved into "the everything store", offering a vast range of products. Amazon owns several major subsidiaries like Amazon Web Services (AWS), Twitch, Ring, Whole Foods Market, and IMDb, and also ventures into areas like cloud computing, autonomous vehicles (Zoox), and satellite internet (Kuiper Systems).

COVID-19

During the COVID-19 pandemic in March 2020, Amazon faced worker protests at its Staten Island warehouse over unsafe conditions; organizer Chris Smalls was fired after raising concerns. Due to a surge in online shopping, Amazon temporarily paused non-essential third-party shipments, hired around 175,000 workers, and raised wages by $2/hour to manage the demand and labor shortage.
