Why Choose Amazon ?

Etsy is the go-to marketplace for unique, handmade, and vintage items, offering millions of personalized and creative products that you won’t find anywhere else. It supports independent artists, crafters, and small businesses, making every purchase more meaningful.

The platform focuses on secure transactions, buyer protection, and direct communication between buyers and sellers, creating a trustworthy and transparent shopping experience. Etsy stands out for promoting sustainable, eco-friendly practices through handmade goods and minimal packaging. It’s a perfect place for gifts, customized items, and rare finds.

Etsy, Inc. is an American e-commerce company that was founded on June 18, 2005, in Brooklyn, New York, by Robert Kalin, Chris Maguire, and Haim Schoppik. Unlike mass retail platforms, Etsy focuses on handcrafted, vintage, and craft supply items—offering a curated space where creative entrepreneurs and small business owners can sell directly to customers.

Etsy’s business model is built on empowering micro-entrepreneurs, and it hosts sellers from all over the world. Over the years, Etsy has grown into one of the largest online marketplaces for artisan-made and customizable goods, maintaining a strong sense of community and purpose.

COVID-19

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Etsy saw a huge surge in demand for handmade face masks, prompting the platform to encourage its sellers to produce and list cloth masks. This helped meet public health needs while supporting small businesses. Etsy sellers adapted quickly to changing consumer needs, and the platform supported them with marketing tools, resources, and fee relief programs during tough times.
