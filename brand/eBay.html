Why Choose eBay?

eBay offers millions of products, both new and used, with competitive pricing and the ability to find unique, hard-to-find items. eBay’s global marketplace allows buyers to access products from sellers worldwide, offering fast shipping options and secure transactions.

eBay provides a customer-focused approach, including buyer protection, easy returns, and a wide variety of payment methods. Innovations like eBay’s authentication program for high-value items and a streamlined mobile experience make shopping convenient and secure. eBay also emphasizes sustainability by supporting the resale of pre-owned products, reducing waste, and promoting a circular economy.

eBay is an American multinational e-commerce corporation, founded in September 1995 by Pierre Omidyar in San Jose, California. Initially started as an online auction site, eBay has grown into a global marketplace offering both auctions and fixed-price listings for various product categories, including electronics, fashion, home goods, collectibles, and more.

eBay operates in over 190 markets worldwide and has acquired various businesses, including PayPal (which was spun off in 2015), and StubHub (event ticketing platform). The company is also heavily involved in promoting small businesses through its marketplace and has a strong focus on supporting the resale and circular economy.

COVID-19

During the COVID-19 pandemic, eBay helped facilitate the sale of essential products, including personal protective equipment (PPE), medical supplies, and groceries, ensuring safe transactions and reliable delivery. The company also supported small businesses by offering financial relief and fee reductions for sellers impacted by the pandemic.

Product Details

eBay provides a wide range of products across various categories, catering to both everyday needs and unique interests. Shoppers can find electronics like mobile phones, laptops, and gaming consoles, as well as fashion items for all age groups. The platform also features home & garden essentials, collectibles and art, and toys & hobbies for enthusiasts. From health & beauty products to automotive parts, sports gear, and business equipment, eBay serves as a versatile marketplace that connects buyers with millions of products from around the world.
